MD. Tariqul Hasan

Assistant General Manager, Operations



Before embarking on his career in the Information Technology (IT) industry, Tariqul Hasan was actively involved in daily news publications and non-governmental organizations (NGOs). His exceptional work history in writing, editing, and reporting for NGOs and daily news outlets laid a solid foundation for his professional journey.

Tariqul began his career at an early age as a staff reporter at The Daily Chand. Following his graduation with a degree in Computer Science and Engineering, he transitioned to the IT sector, where his passion for technology and media merged.

He received intensive training on the Principles of Electronic Journalism from Sarah Communication (ETV), enhancing his expertise in media and communication. Additionally, he earned several diplomas in diverse fields, including:

  • Graphics Design
  • Macromedia Flash
  • Video Editing
  • Adobe Premiere
  • Final Cut Pro
  • Ulead Video Studio
  • Pinnacle
  • Edius

His educational background also includes a Diploma in Electronics, a Certificate in Filmmaking and Television Production, as well as certifications in CCNA and SCSA. Tariqul has been closely associated with the Human Rights Monitoring Organization (HRMO) since its inception, contributing significantly to its mission.

Tariqul Hasan is well-recognized for his excellent skills in writing, editing, and oral communication in English. He possesses outstanding human relations abilities, a keen creative and analytical sense, and is adept at planning, managing, and motivating teams. His expertise also extends to training and providing clear explanations.

With exceptional interpersonal skills and the ability to work effectively in multicultural and multi-ethnic environments, Tariqul demonstrates a strong work ethic, integrity, and a forward-thinking mindset. He continuously seeks to enhance and modernize existing systems.

Currently, Tariqul Hasan holds the position of Senior Manager, Operations at FIFOTech, where he continues to drive operational excellence and innovation.
