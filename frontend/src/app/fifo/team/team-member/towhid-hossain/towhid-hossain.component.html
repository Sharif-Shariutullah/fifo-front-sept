Towhid Hossain

Managing Director & CEO



Since 2004, FIFOTech has been offering Information Technology Services, Business Process Outsourcing (BPO) Solutions, Supply Chain Management, Consultancy Services, Financial Accounting Services, and Social Media Marketing. Leveraging our industry expertise and global offshore presence, we ensure the delivery of high-quality, low-cost BPO solutions to our clients, maximizing value and efficiency.

Towhid Hossain, the Managing Director and CEO, embarked on a remarkable journey that shaped his career in the ITES/BPO/Telco sector. He possesses extensive and diverse experience, having worked for renowned multinational companies such as GrameenPhone, WorldTel, and Noor-Bangla, among many other prestigious firms. His expertise also extends to the Contact Center Industry in Bangladesh, where he has held direct roles in companies like FIFOTech, Eva Telecommunications, A2Z BPO, and Virgo Contact Center Services Ltd.

Due to his unparalleled contributions to the BPO industry, Towhid Hossain has served in several key positions within the Bangladesh Association of Call Center and Outsourcing (BACCO). He held the position of Finance Secretary (2013-2015), Secretary General (2015-2017), and is currently serving in the same role (2017-2019). Ref: BACCO Committee

With experience managing over 7,000 people across various ventures, Towhid has also become a renowned motivational speaker. He has delivered more than 500 sessions at major private and public educational institutions, focusing on Employment, Career Development, and Communication.

He is widely recognized as a pioneer of Women Empowerment in Bangladesh. His contributions include leading the National Hackathon for Women and serving as an Advisor for Girls in ICT.

Towhid Hossain is actively involved in both public and private sector projects. He has worked with the Bangladesh Technical Education Board (BTEB) and the International Labor Organization (ILO) on skills development and training programs, serving as a panelist for the ICT sector. He also plays a significant role as Treasurer in the Industries Skills Council (ISC), ICT under the National Skills Development Council (NSDC). Ref: NSDC

He is currently engaged with various government organizations on multiple projects involving the ICT Ministry, ADB, World Bank, a2i, Ministry of Education, Home Ministry, and Ministry of Finance.

In addition to his corporate roles, Towhid Hossain is the Founder Director & Organizing Secretary of Serve Bangladesh Society (an NGO involved in the education sector), and the Director of Communication for the Human Rights Monitoring Organization (HRMO). He also serves as an executive member of the Bangladesh Alliance for Fair Competition (BAFCOM).

Furthermore, Towhid is an active member of the Federation of Bangladesh Chambers of Commerce & Industries (FBCCI), serving on various committees as co-chairman and member. He has made key contributions to the e-commerce industry in Bangladesh and currently holds the position of Chairman of the Service Delivery Committee for e-CAB. Ref: FBCCI Members
