Md Obaidul Haque

General Manager, Finance & Admin



Md Obaidul Haque, an Ex-Army officer, began his distinguished career serving the Bangladesh Government from a young age. His disciplined approach and strong sense of responsibility have made him a valuable member of the FIFOTech team since he joined in 2018 as the Account Manager.

Known for his punctuality and dedication, Obaidul has consistently maintained high standards in managing his responsibilities. His professionalism and reliability have made him a senior and respected member of the FIFOTech family.

Since joining FIFOTech, Obaidul has played a crucial role in ensuring the financial and operational integrity of the company. His contributions have been key to the company's continued success, and his leadership continues to inspire his colleagues.
